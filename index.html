Experience

I have worked as a Solution Architect at Tridiagonal Solutions, focusing on generative AI solutions, voice-to-text integration, and real-time 3D avatar services. Prior to that, I was a Software Engineer at Youngsoft India, developing ergonomic assessment software, AI-driven IoT systems, and data processing pipelines. My earlier internships gave me exposure to educational application development, inventory management systems, and Databricks-based data processing. Over the years, I've embraced a variety of technologies including Python, OpenCV, AWS, Azure, and more to build scalable, intelligent applications for diverse industries.
